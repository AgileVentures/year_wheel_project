# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 15, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to YearLine ("we", "us", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use YearLine.

YearLine is provided by **Communitas Labs Inc**, a company incorporated in Delaware, United States.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information from monday.com
When you use YearLine, we access:
- **Board Data**: Groups, items, tasks, dates, status, and assignments (read/write access)
- **User Information**: Your name and email from your monday.com account
- **Account Context**: Board IDs, workspace information, and session data

**Important**: This data is accessed in real-time through monday.com's APIs and is NOT permanently stored on our servers.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
We collect minimal technical data:
- **Usage Analytics**: Which features you use (anonymized)
- **Session Data**: Temporary session tokens for authentication
- **Error Logs**: Technical errors for debugging (no personal data)

### 2.3 What We Don't Collect
We do NOT collect or store:
- Your board data permanently
- Task details, descriptions, or content
- File attachments or documents
- Sensitive business information
- Payment information (YearLine is currently free)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:
- **Display Timelines**: Show your monday.com data as visual timelines
- **Enable Editing**: Allow drag-and-drop rescheduling that syncs to monday.com
- **Provide Support**: Help troubleshoot issues when you contact us
- **Improve Service**: Understand which features are used (anonymized analytics)
- **Ensure Security**: Detect and prevent unauthorized access

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:
- **Consent**: You authorize YearLine to access your monday.com boards
- **Legitimate Interest**: To provide the timeline visualization service you requested
- **Contract**: To fulfill our agreement with you when you use YearLine

## 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

### 5.1 Who We Share Data With

We share data with minimal third parties:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared | Your Control |
|---------|---------|-------------|--------------|
| **monday.com** | Source of all board data | API requests with board IDs | Revoke access in monday.com settings |
| **Analytics** | Usage patterns (anonymized) | Page views, feature clicks | None (fully anonymized) |

### 5.2 What We Don't Share
We do NOT:
- Sell your data to advertisers
- Share board data with other users
- Use your data for marketing purposes
- Share data with data brokers

### 5.3 monday.com Platform
- All board data remains in monday.com's infrastructure
- YearLine accesses data through monday.com's secure OAuth 2.0 APIs
- Your data is subject to monday.com's Privacy Policy and security measures
- We don't control monday.com's data processing or storage

## 6. Data Storage and Security

### 6.1 Where Your Data Lives
- **Board Data**: Stored in monday.com (not in YearLine servers)
- **Session Tokens**: Temporarily stored for authentication (encrypted)
- **User Metadata**: Your name and email cached temporarily during sessions

### 6.2 Security Measures
We implement:
- **Encryption**: All data transmission via HTTPS/TLS
- **OAuth 2.0**: Secure authentication through monday.com
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to production systems
- **Regular Audits**: Security reviews and updates

### 6.3 Data Retention
- **Board Data**: Not retained (accessed in real-time from monday.com)
- **Session Data**: Deleted when you log out or after 24 hours
- **Error Logs**: Retained for 30 days for debugging
- **Analytics**: Anonymized data retained indefinitely

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### 7.1 GDPR Rights (EU Users)
You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request what data we have about you
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data (update in monday.com)
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data
- **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data
- **Portability**: Export your data (available through monday.com)
- **Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw Consent**: Revoke YearLine's access anytime

### 7.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)
You have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect
- Request deletion of your information
- Opt-out of data selling (we don't sell data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

### 7.3 How to Exercise Your Rights
To exercise your rights:
1. **Revoke Access**: Remove YearLine from your monday.com board settings
2. **Contact Us**: Email hey@communitaslabs.io with your request
3. **monday.com Data**: Manage your board data through monday.com

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

### 8.1 Cookies We Use
- **Session Cookies**: Keep you logged in (deleted on logout)
- **Analytics Cookies**: Understand feature usage (anonymized)

### 8.2 Your Cookie Choices
- Disable cookies in your browser (may affect functionality)
- Use incognito/private browsing mode
- Clear cookies regularly

## 9. International Data Transfers
