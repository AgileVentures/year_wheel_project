## Color Scheme Guide

### Score Buttons:
- **Scores 0-6 (Detractors):** 
  - Default: `bg-gray-300`
  - Selected: `bg-red-500` with ring
  - Hover: `bg-red-600`

- **Scores 7-8 (Passives):**
  - Default: `bg-gray-300`
  - Selected: `bg-yellow-500` with ring
  - Hover: `bg-yellow-600`

- **Scores 9-10 (Promoters):**
  - Default: `bg-gray-300`
  - Selected: `bg-green-500` with ring
  - Hover: `bg-green-600`

- **Promoter Badge:** `bg-green-100 text-green-800`
- **Passive Badge:** `bg-yellow-100 text-yellow-800`
